Privacy Policy

Migraine Tracker — personal health logging app

What data we collect

Migraine Tracker collects information you enter about your headaches: dates, duration, pain levels, symptoms, treatments, and notes. It also optionally collects your device's GPS coordinates if you choose to record your location for an incident.

Where your data is stored

All of your data is stored exclusively in your own Google Drive, in a folder named MigraineTracker/. No data is ever transmitted to, processed by, or stored on any third-party server. The app runs entirely in your browser.

Google account access

This app requests the drive.file permission scope from Google. This allows the app to read and write only the files that it creates itself — it cannot access any other files in your Google Drive.

Your Google account credentials are never stored by this app. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's Identity Services.

Who can see your data

Only you, through your Google account. Because the data lives in your personal Google Drive, it is subject to Google's own privacy and security practices.

Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the MigraineTracker/ folder from your Google Drive. The app provides a direct link to Google Drive in the Settings screen.

Cookies and tracking

This app does not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technology. The only browser storage used is localStorage to cache your Google Drive folder ID so the app loads faster — no personal health data is stored locally.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will appear here. Because this is a personal-use app, changes are unlikely.
